How to Use DeepFlow Pro Timer

Your User ID

1

Remember Your User ID

Your unique 6-digit User ID is displayed at the top of the app. This ID is important for saving and loading your data from the cloud.

2

Save Your User ID

Write down your User ID somewhere safe. If you lose it, you won't be able to access your saved data. You can generate a new ID, but this will create a new profile.

Important

Your User ID is the key to your data. Without it, you cannot retrieve your saved sessions, notes, or coins. Keep it safe!

Using the Timer

1

Select Focus Duration

Choose your desired focus session length from the preset buttons (15, 25, 30, 45, or 60 minutes).

2

Start the Timer

Click the "Start" button to begin your focus session. The circular progress indicator will show your progress.

3

Take Breaks

After each focus session, take a short break (5 minutes by default). After 4 sessions, take a longer break (15 minutes by default).

4

Earn Coins

You earn 0.5 coins for every hour of focused work. Use these coins to unlock premium features in the Rewards Shop.

Managing Notes

1

Add Notes

Type your task or note in the text area and click "Add" or press Enter to save it.

2

Delete Notes

Click the "X" button on any note to delete it.

3

Save Notes to Cloud

Click the "Save" button in the notes section to store your notes in the cloud.

4

Load Notes from Cloud

Click the "Load" button to retrieve your saved notes from the cloud using your User ID.

Data Management

1

Save Data to Cloud

Click the "Sync" button in the timer controls to save your session data, notes, and settings to the cloud.

2

Load Data from Cloud

Click the "Load" button in the session history section to retrieve your data from the cloud.

3

Clear History

Use the "Clear" button to reset your session history and statistics.

Focus Music

1

Select Music

Choose from various focus music options like Lofi Beats, Classical, Rain Sounds, Motivation Mix, or Quran.

2

Play Music

Click on any music option to start playing it in the embedded player.
